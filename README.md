=== Abstract ===

The Aquaculture, science of growing organisms that live in aquatic environments is one of the most important productive aspects of Brazil. This area tends to grow over the years because of its profitability and scalability compared to other types of productive culture. However, there are weaknesses that can undermine an entire production and at this juncture is that technology can contribute. The development of cloud hypothetical application can make more efficient production based on analysis of data, thus promoting the aid in making decisions regarding the manufacturing.
